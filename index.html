benefits

key benefits to becoming a member of the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE)

Networking
Opportunities

SAE provides opportunities for members to connect with other professionals in the automotive and mobility industries, including engineers, researchers, and industry leaders. These connections can lead to new business opportunities, career growth, and valuable partnerships.

Access to
Technical Information

Members of SAE have access to a wealth of technical information, including industry research, technical papers, and standards. This information can help members stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in automotive technology and make informed decisions about product design and development.

Professional
Development

SAE offers a range of professional development opportunities, including training courses, webinars, and conferences. These resources can help members enhance their skills and knowledge, stay current with industry trends, and advance their careers.

Industry
Recognition

SAE membership can enhance an individual's credibility and reputation in the automotive and mobility industries. Membership in SAE demonstrates a commitment to professional excellence and a dedication to advancing the industry.

Discounts on SAE
Products and Services

Members of SAE receive discounts on a range of products and services, including publications, conferences, and professional development courses. These discounts can help members save money while staying up-to-date with the latest industry information and trends.

Leadership
Opportunities

SAE offers leadership opportunities for members who want to take an active role in the organization. Members can serve on committees, participate in working groups, and take on leadership roles within the organization. These opportunities can help members build their leadership skills and make a positive impact on the industry.

ACHIEVEMENTS

These awards are a testament to SAE's commitment to advancing the knowledge and practice of engineering in the automotive and mobility industries, promoting safety, sustainability, and innovation. SAE continues to work towards driving progress in the field and making a positive impact on the industry.

ASTM International President's Leadership Award

SAE received this award in 2021 in recognition of its leadership in developing technical standards for the mobility industry.

American Society of Association Executives (ASAE) Power of A Gold Award

SAE received this award in 2020 for its efforts to promote diversity and inclusion in the automotive and mobility industries.

Prince Michael International Road Safety Award

SAE received this award in 2019 for its work in promoting road safety through the development of technical standards and best practices.

Automotive News PACE Award

SAE received this award in 2018 for its development of the Automated and Connected Vehicle Systems Testing Symposium.

World Standards Cooperation (WSC) Certificate of Merit

SAE received this award in 2017 for its contributions to the development of international standards for the automotive and mobility industries.

About Us

The Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing the knowledge and practice of engineering in the automotive and mobility industries. Founded in 1905, SAE is an international organization with over 200,000 members worldwide, including engineers, researchers, technicians, educators, and students.

SAE provides a platform for individuals and companies in the automotive industry to collaborate, exchange knowledge, and advance technological innovation. The organization is involved in a wide range of activities, including developing technical standards, promoting research and development, and hosting events and conferences.
